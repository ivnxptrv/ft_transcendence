# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what data Vekko ("we") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. Vekko is an educational project built as part of the 42 school curriculum.

## 2. Information We Collect

- **Account information** — your email address, optional first and last name, and a password (stored only as a bcrypt hash). If you sign in with Google, we store your Google account identifier.
- **Security data** — if you enable two-factor authentication, a TOTP secret and hashed single-use recovery codes.
- **Content you provide** — insider "legend" text, order titles and text, and insight text.
- **Marketplace & wallet data** — your matches, wallet balance, and transaction history (top-ups, withdrawals, and purchases).
- **Technical data** — short-lived authentication cookies (access and refresh tokens) and basic server logs.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- create, authenticate, and secure your account;
- operate the marketplace, including automatically matching orders to insiders using text embeddings and cosine similarity;
- process in-app wallet transactions; and
- maintain security and prevent abuse.

## 4. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies to store your authentication tokens and keep you signed in. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

## 5. Third Parties

If you choose Google sign-in, authentication is handled by Google under its own privacy policy. We do not sell your personal data.

## 6. How Your Data Is Processed

Vekko runs as several internal services (identity, marketplace, matching, and ledger). These services reference you by a stable internal identifier rather than your email, and exchange only the data needed to provide the Service.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your account and content data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service.

## 8. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting the Vekko project team.

## 9. Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt, sessions use signed (RS256) JSON Web Tokens with rotation and revocation, and two-factor authentication is available. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

## 10. Children

The Service is not directed to children and is intended for use by adults in an educational context.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

## 12. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Vekko project team.
